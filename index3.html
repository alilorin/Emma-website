About English Advantage

English Advantage was founded in October, 2015 by instructor Emma Sheppard. Classes are in small groups, or one on one and are designed to address the difficulties and needs of each and every student. Our classes are designed to take your English skills to the next level, increase confidence, improve pronunciation, and correct common, persistent errors.

The goal of this project is to incorporate the personal experiences and motivations of all students into the class and create a space where everyone feels confident in their language ability while also being pushed and challenged to gain and refine new skills. English Advantage prides itself on flexibility and a personal approach, we will work with you to make sure you are learning what you want the way you want. So, come advance your English with English Advantage!

Emma's Bio

Emma has been teaching ESL for eight years, and has taught in 5 countries and worked with students from all over the world, with all different levels of English knowledge. In 2014 she received a Masters of Arts in Education from the University of Toronto. For the past few years she has focused on the needs of advanced learners. Her goal is to incorporate the personal experiences and motivations of all students into her teaching and create a space where each and every student feels confident in their language ability, no matter what level, while also being pushed and challenged to gain and refine new skills.
